About IIPS

Technology With Real-World Intent

IIPS develops digital infrastructure, financial systems, and smart commerce tools designed to solve real-world problems across payments, education, retail, and digital services.

Company Mission

Empowering individuals, schools, and businesses with cutting-edge technology and innovative digital solutions.

Every IIPS product is designed to be understandable, practical, and aligned with the constraints of the environments where it will operate.

Company Vision

Together, our projects form a broader ecosystem aimed at building digital financial infrastructure, smart commerce tools, secure payment systems, and operational platforms for businesses.

The mission of IIPS is to create technology solutions that simplify real-world operations while maintaining transparency, security, and efficiency.

Our Technology Approach

IIPS platforms are engineered around a few non‑negotiable principles: reliability, observability, security, and usability in bandwidth-constrained environments.

  • RWS (Real World System) — blockchain infrastructure connecting real-world assets, banks, and merchants.
  • Use My Card — secure payment-sharing without exposing full card details.
  • Invify & Bar Project — smart invoicing and hospitality management, including offline-first capability.
  • IIPS Monitoring App — internal tool for server health, uptime, and performance visibility.

Legal & Compliance

IIPS understands that sustainable technology must align with legal frameworks and regulatory expectations from the very beginning.

Structured Review

All major projects undergo structured legal review before submission to relevant authorities, ensuring clarity of scope, data handling, and operational risk.

Regulatory Touchpoints

Depending on the solution, IIPS may engage with bodies such as the Ministry of Justice, the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC), and the National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA).

Documentation & Audit Trails

Systems are instrumented with logging, audit trails, and documentation to make regulatory engagement transparent and efficient.

Future Roadmap

IIPS is building a portfolio of connected platforms that reinforce each other over time.

RWS — Real World System

Full rollout of blockchain-powered infrastructure for asset tokenization, bank and merchant nodes, digital contracts, and regulatory-compliant financial operations.

Use My Card

Expansion of the payment-sharing platform with broader card network support and enhanced spending controls for families, teams, and remote purchases.

Invify & Bar Project

Scaling smart invoicing and hospitality management with school billing, term-based invoices, and bar/lounge digital operations for small and medium businesses.

Let Us Bet & IIPS Monitoring

Launch of the social challenge platform with escrow and validation, plus expanded monitoring capabilities for all IIPS systems and partner infrastructure.
