IIPS Major Projects

Digital infrastructure, financial systems, and smart commerce tools solving real-world problems across payments, education, retail, and digital services.

RWS — Real World System

Blockchain-powered infrastructure connecting real-world assets, businesses, and financial services into a transparent digital ecosystem.

  • Asset tokenization for businesses
  • Bank and merchant node systems
  • Digital contracts & ownership verification
  • Regulatory-compliant operations

Use My Card

Payment-sharing platform that lets users securely allow someone else to use their payment card without exposing full card details.

  • Card owner approves transactions
  • Spending limits & duration controls
  • Traceable transactions
  • Parents, friends, remote purchases

Let Us Bet Project

Structured social betting and challenge platform for peer-to-peer wagers around events or personal challenges.

  • Sports prediction & fitness challenges
  • Escrow-based bet locking
  • Transparent result validation
  • Community-driven challenges

Bar Project

Smart hospitality and retail management system for bars, lounges, and beverage businesses.

  • Digital ordering & inventory
  • Table service management
  • Sales analytics
  • Staff management

Invify Project

Smart invoicing and transaction management for businesses, merchants, and service providers. Offline-first.

  • Professional invoices & payment tracking
  • School billing & term-based invoices
  • Products, services, financial reports
  • Works without internet

IIPS Monitoring App

Internal infrastructure tool for monitoring health and performance of IIPS systems and servers.

  • Server uptime & API performance
  • Storage status & error logs
  • Real-time alerts
  • Cloud & backend visibility

Reliable Monitoring & Backend Systems

IIPS designs infrastructure with resilience and observability at the core. Every request, message, and asset is orchestrated across a robust hybrid cloud environment.

  • Contabo servers for high-availability compute and edge workloads.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging for real-time notifications and event delivery.
  • Supabase for secure, scalable relational data with APIs.
  • Hybrid cloud architecture that balances cost, redundancy, and performance.
